So what is
Friday Shoes?



Friday Shoes is a band from Auburn, Alabama mostly specializing in covers,
with a range in styles from funk to pop to indie to rock. We are open to accepting gigs anywhere within a handfull of hours of Auburn. Members include:

Jack Grant, John Sturgill, Peter Anthony, & Ethan Whittington.
